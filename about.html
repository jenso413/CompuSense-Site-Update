Our History

CompuSense was established in 1998 by Ed Lipp Jr. shortly after graduating high school in 1997. He started CompuSense as a way to use his computer skills and help others learn to use their equipment to its fullest. In the beginning he was focused on building web sites and custom built computers; those two service eventually morphed in to providing on-site services for the greater Cleveland area.
